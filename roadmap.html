Public join page
/join/[slug] — public discovery page with community info. Players can request to join with a message. Admins approve or reject from the Players tab.

Co-admin role
Admins can promote players to co-admin. Same rights as owner except deleting the community.
Done

Google Maps link and price per person on each tournament. Shown as badges on the detail page.

Date/time picker on creation. Shown as a badge on the tournament detail page.

Level badges (D → A)
7-level system: D, D+, C, C+, B, B+, A with official descriptions. Shown as compact badges throughout the app.
